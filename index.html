Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Founded in 2009 as Hari Fashions, the company evolved into Hari Tex in 2022, strengthening its identity as a quality-driven woven fabric manufacturer headquartered in Salem, Tamil Nadu.

Over the years, Hari Tex has earned a solid reputation for product integrity, execution reliability, and buyer trust. We combine traditional textile craftsmanship with modern weaving technology to serve global markets.
